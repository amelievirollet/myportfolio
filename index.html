Follow Me

Hi, my name is Virollet Amélie, I am 21 years old, and I am currently a student in marketing at Kedge Business School (campus of Paris). My fascination with photography dates back to my childhood, influenced by my father who was a passionate amateur of this visual art. In November 2020, I decided to immerse myself fully in this passion that had been dormant within me for a long time. I began my journey with my father's old analog camera, a Canon EOS 300X. From then on, I fell in love with the art of capturing moments and emotions through my lens.
